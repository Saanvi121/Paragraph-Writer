Paragraph Writer: Your handy sentence connector

How to use:-

Type your sentences in the 5 boxes for paragraph 1 column to get a paragraph. Enter the rest of your text's sentences in the boxes provided in paragraph 2's column. Once you click the frame paragraph buttons, one by one your paragraphs will be diplayed.
